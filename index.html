A Serious Issue Between Ravi And Ram

Question:-

After a long planning, two friends Ravi and Gopi decided to go to a movie on holiday, cool !! right. But here is the problem, Both of them are having different opinions on 2 different movies and are unable to deicde on which one to go, so they came up with a rock paper scissors game. Where they will play 'n' number of games and the wining one will choose the movie to goo.. please help them to sort out this issue by telling them the result.

So here are the rules.

There will be n rounds and for each round one can chosse [ Rock - R or Scissors - S or Paper - P ] all are in Capital.

1) Rock (R) beats Scissors (S).
2) Scissors (S) beats Paper (P).
3) Paper (P) beats Rock (R).

Input Format :-


First Line :- number of rounds (n).
Second Line :- (n) number of moves by Ravi each consists of ( R or S or P ).
Third Line :- (n) number of moves by Gopi each consists of ( R or S or P ).
print "Ravi wins." if Ravi wins without double quotation marks
print "Gopi wins." if Gopi wins without double quotation marks
print "No Result Play Agian." if its a draw

Sample Input :-

3
R R R
S P S

Sample Output :-

Ravi Wins

Explanation :-


* In first round ravi chooses Rock(R) and Gopi chooses (S) so Ravi Wins first round.
* In Second round ravi chooses Rock(R) and Gopi chooses (p) so Gopi Wins Second round
* In Final round ravi chooses Rock(R) and Gopi chooses (S) so Ravi Wins Final round.
* So Ravi wins 2 round and Gopi Wins 1 round so the output is Ravi wins.
